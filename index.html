About Sawata Research Hub

Sawata Research Hub is the official institutional repository of Sawata National High School, dedicated to preserving, organizing, and providing accessible digital access to research outputs produced by our academic community.

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive, searchable digital archive that preserves the intellectual heritage of Sawata National High School while enhancing research accessibility, promoting academic continuity, and fostering a culture of evidence-based inquiry among students and faculty.

Our Vision

To be the leading institutional repository among secondary schools in Davao Region, serving as a model for digital knowledge management and inspiring future generations of student researchers and innovators.

Objectives

  • Systematically archive all research studies, capstone projects, and Science Investigatory Projects conducted at SNHS
  • Provide a user-friendly platform for searching and accessing research materials
  • Facilitate literature reviews and reduce research duplication
  • Showcase the quality and diversity of research outputs to stakeholders
  • Promote academic continuity and knowledge sharing within the school community
